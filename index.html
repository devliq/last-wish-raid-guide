⚔️
Mechanics
Kalli, the Corrupted
1
Match the 6 Plates
Six symbols float around the center arena. Match each plate's symbol to one of the six. Assign one player per matching plate.
2
Stand on Plates + Kill Knights
All players stand on their assigned plate. A Taken Knight spawns near each — kill all six to begin the DPS phase on Kalli.
3
DPS Phase
Move to center and burn Kalli. When she readies her Ontological Weapon, six doors open around the room — all players must enter a door or wipe.
4
Repeat Until Dead
Kalli is typically one-phased with good DPS. Equip 3 Surge mods + 3 Reserve mods matching your heavy weapon element.
👥
Role Assignments
6-player fireteam
× 6
Plate Holder
Each player takes one of the six plates. Identify your symbol before the encounter starts.
× 1
Well / Rift
Warlock drops Well of Radiance during DPS phase. Everyone stacks inside for damage buff.
× 1
Ward Caller
Calls out when Kalli preps her weapon so team enters doors in time. Watch her animation.

Challenge: Summoning Ritual — summon and kill all three Ogres before each DPS phase.

✅
Pre-Encounter Checklist
Tick off before pulling aggro
  • Equip 3× Surge mods matching heavy weapon
  • Equip 3× Reserve mods matching heavy weapon
  • Learn your plate symbol before encounter start
  • Warlock has Well of Radiance ready (or Rifts for DPS)
  • Highest burst-damage Super equipped for DPS phase
  • Doors noted — identify nearest door before DPS starts

⚔️
Mechanics
Shuro Chi, the Corrupted
1
3 Floors, 6 DPS Phases
Shuro Chi's health is divided into 6 segments. Two DPS phases per floor across three floors. Break each health bar segment.
2
Tic-Tac-Toe Plate Puzzle
Three rooms per floor, each has a 3×3 grid of symbols. Runners stand on the three plates matching the center device's symbols. Call middle symbol first.
3
Kill Eye of Riven
After puzzle, the Eye of Riven spawns. Kill it and collect the Taken Essence. Use the grenade ability to trigger DPS phase. Don't let the song timer expire!
4
Penumbral vs Antumbral
Eye of Riven drops either Penumbral or Antumbral Taken Essence. Note which one — it must be popped on the matching plate type.
👥
Role Assignments
Stairs · Trees · Rocks layout
× 3
Runner
One Runner per room (Stairs, Trees, Rocks). Stand on all 3 plates, call middle symbol, solve the plate puzzle each cycle.
× 3
Gunner
One Gunner per room. Clear ads for the Runner. Pair with a Runner and defend them throughout each floor.
× 2
DPS Lead
Coordinates when to pop Taken Essence and call DPS phase. Usually Runners from last active room after escape.

Challenge: Which Witch — never get hit by Shuro Chi's long-range Arc attack.

✅
Pre-Encounter Checklist
  • Know your room assignment (Stairs / Trees / Rocks)
  • Know your role: Runner or Gunner
  • Understand Penumbral vs Antumbral plate matching
  • Note when the music song timer is nearly expired
  • Hunter Tether or Titan Banner Shield for DPS phases
  • Know which floor you're switching to after each DPS
🔮
Pro Tips
💡
Stick to Your Plate
Remember which plate you stood on — if you're off it in the tic-tac-toe phase, hop back on fast. Coordination wipes here more than anything else.
💡
Forced Room Mechanic
After solving, a force-field traps the Runner in their room. Other players call out which room is now open so the Runner knows which tunnel to take.
💡
Hidden Chest
First hidden chest is below the bridge just before this encounter. Find it for a chance at raid mods.
⚔️
Mechanics
Morgeth, the Spirekeeper
1
Taken Strength Stacking
Taken Strength orbs spawn throughout the arena. You can hold a maximum of 2 at a time — grabbing a 3rd kills you instantly. Plan carefully.
2
Designated Carrier
Assign one player to pick up the FIRST Taken Strength and the LAST one. Their purpose is to never touch another until the final collection.
3
Left Side Collection
After first Taken Strength is grabbed, carrier runs to the left side. Other players collect additional orbs, rotating when at max stacks.
4
Stagger & DPS
Collect enough Taken Strength to stagger Morgeth. During stagger, all players unload maximum DPS. Rinse and repeat until dead.
👥
Role Assignments
× 1
First/Last Carrier
Picks up first Taken Strength and reserves the last. Must not touch any other orb between these two events. Critical role.
× 3–4
Orb Collectors
Collect Taken Strength orbs, staying at or below 2 stacks. Hand off to the next player when maxed. Call your stack count.
× 1
Well / Support
Warlock drops Well during DPS windows. Also calls stagger timing so team knows when to stack and burst.

Challenge: Forever Flight — do NOT kill any Ogres that spawn during this encounter.

✅
Pre-Encounter Checklist
  • Assign First/Last Carrier role before starting
  • All players know the 2-stack max rule (3 = instant death)
  • Good ad-clear weapon equipped (Morgeth spawns many ads)
  • For challenge: agree NOT to shoot Ogres
  • Hidden chest location scouted (across large gap, before this encounter)
🔮
Key Notes
⚠️
3 Stacks = Instant Wipe
The most common cause of death here. Call out your current stack count constantly. If you see 2 stacks on yourself, move away from orbs immediately.
💡
Power Creep Makes This Easy
Morgeth can usually be one-phased in the current meta. Focus on collecting enough orbs to get one stagger and dump everything into the boss.
⚔️
Mechanics
Open the Vault
1
Three Rooms, Three Locations
The Vault has three locations: Stairs (left), Trees (middle), and Rocks (right). Same layout as Shuro Chi. Assign teams of two to each.
2
Identify Open Room
A Taken wall blocks 2 of the 3 rooms. One room is always open. The player whose room is open enters, kills the Eye of Riven, and takes the Essence.
3
Force Field Rotation
After taking the Essence, a force field traps the Runner. Others tell them which room is now open. Runner takes the side tunnel to escape to the open room.
4
Pop on Matching Plate × 2
Repeat the cycle twice — collect two Essences total, popping each on its matching plate to open the vault. Then DPS the boss.
👥
Role Assignments
× 3
Runner
One Runner per room. Enters open room, kills Eye, takes Essence. Navigates tunnel to next open room when trapped.
× 3
Gunner
Defends their Runner. Calls which room is unblocked after force-field swap. Heavy ad-clear focus.
× 1+
Knight Killer
For the challenge: kill all Might of Riven Knights before they reach the center. Assign fastest-DPS players.

Challenge: Keep Out — kill all Might of Riven Knights before they reach the chamber center.

✅
Pre-Encounter Checklist
  • Assign Stairs / Trees / Rocks room assignments
  • Know which tunnel connects to which room
  • Understand Penumbral vs Antumbral plate distinction
  • Heavy DPS weapons ready for vault boss encounter
  • Survival loadout on Runners — survivability over raw DPS

Mechanics
Riven of a Thousand Voices
1
Split Into Two Rooms
Six players split into two rooms of three. Each room has Riven appearing — look for her eyes. Three players per room. There's a hidden starting room — run right from spawn, jump down 2 floors.
2
Shoot the Eyes
Riven shows 2 eyes per stage, then more. Each guardian in the room memorizes which eyes they hit. For the Challenge: never shoot the same eye twice.
3
DPS Phase — Mouth
When Riven opens her mouth, unload all DPS into it. She has 4 stages of eye reveals. Final stage has a black ball in her mouth — that's your main damage window.
4
Queenswalk — Carry the Heart
After Riven dies: the Fates Chosen carries the Heart with a countdown timer. Call the last 10 seconds. When teleported to Eternity, non-carriers pick up 1 blight ASAP to extend timer. Escort heart to the dunking point.
👥
Role Assignments
× 3
Room A Team
Three guardians in left Riven room. Each memorizes which eyes they shoot across all 4 stages. Do not re-shoot your eyes for the challenge.
× 3
Room B Team
Three guardians in right Riven room. Same rules. Communicate with Room A team via callouts — Riven's rooms are mirrored.
× 1
Blight Keeper
During Queenswalk, responsible for collecting the final blight to extend timer. Designated first teleported player. Most important Queenswalk role.
× 1
Heart Carrier
Fates Chosen — carries Riven's Heart. Calls timer ("10 seconds!"). Move with the group toward dunk point. Everyone protects the carrier.

Challenge: Strength of Memory — never shoot the same Riven eye more than once.

✅
Pre-Encounter Checklist
  • Assign Room A and Room B teams (3 per room)
  • Designate Blight Keeper for Queenswalk
  • Best DPS loadout equipped — swords + heavy weapons
  • Everyone knows the hidden starting room path
  • Plan first teleport order for Queenswalk
  • Challenges active? Note eyes you shoot — no repeat shots
🏆
Riven Drop Info
One Thousand Voices
Solar Exotic Fusion Rifle. 5% base drop rate from Riven. Completing raid challenges improves odds. Most coveted exotic in Last Wish.
💡
Queenswalk Countdown
The toughest Queenswalk moment is the final seconds. Pick up a blight immediately when teleported to Eternity — this saves you scrambling at the last second.
💡
Blight Keeper Timing
Blight Keeper should pick up the last Taken Strength as late as possible into the timer — this maximizes the time extension for the outside carrier player.

Challenges

Weekly Challenges

Complete for bonus drops and improved One Thousand Voices odds. Click to track completion.

⚔️
Summoning Ritual
Encounter 1 · Kalli, the Corrupted
Summon and kill all three Ogres that appear before each DPS phase. Do not skip them — they must be defeated before engaging Kalli in damage.
Extra drops + increased 1KV odds
🏃
Which Witch
Encounter 2 · Shuro Chi, the Corrupted
Stay on the move for the entire fight. Avoid Shuro Chi's long-range Arc tracking attack — it will kill you if you're stationary. Never stop moving when she targets you.
Extra drops + increased 1KV odds
🦷
Forever Flight
Encounter 3 · Morgeth, the Spirekeeper
Do not kill any Ogres that spawn during the Morgeth encounter. These are different from Kalli's Ogres — they are environmental. Ignore them entirely and focus only on Morgeth and the Taken Strength orbs.
Extra drops + increased 1KV odds
🔒
Keep Out
Encounter 4 · Open the Vault
Kill all Might of Riven Knights before they reach the center of the vault chamber. Designate fast-DPS players to intercept knights immediately — any reaching the center fails the challenge.
Extra drops + increased 1KV odds
👁️
Strength of Memory
Encounter 5 · Riven of a Thousand Voices
Never shoot the same Riven eye more than once across all four stages. Each player must memorize and track every eye they personally shoot. Use callouts to coordinate with your room team.
Extra drops + improved 1KV odds

Raid Mods

Found by looting the two secret chests. First: below the bridge before Shuro Chi. Second: across the large gap before Morgeth.

Taken Armaments
Defeating a Taken combatant with a grenade grants Heavy ammo. Cannot stack across multiple copies.
Source: Secret chests
Taken Barrer
Receiving Taken damage gives a 20% damage reduction for 10 seconds. Excellent for all encounters.
Source: Secret chests
Taken Invigoration
Defeating a challenging Taken combatant refreshes your class ability. Cannot stack.
Source: Secret chests
Taken Repurposing
Destroying a Taken shield refreshes your grenade ability. Great for Shuro Chi and Vault encounter.
Source: Secret chests
Wish Wall

Ahamkara Wishes

15 wishes activate at the wall before any encounter. Input sequences using the 4 animal symbols.

How to use: The wish wall is accessed from the starting area before any encounter. Use 4 directional inputs with the 4 Ahamkara animal symbols to enter each wish. Each wish persists until the raid instance resets.

Wish 1
Grant Me a Wish
Spawns a chest at the end of the raid with a random item. Best used to get a free attempt at a specific weapon.

Checkpoint Skip

Wanderer's Respite
